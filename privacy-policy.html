AutoMiles Privacy Addendum

This Addendum applies only to the AutoMiles application developed by DNNR Tech and supplements the main Privacy Policy above.

AutoMiles is a drive and trip tracking app designed to automatically record driving activity and build a complete driving history for users.

Location Data Usage

AutoMiles uses device location data to enable automatic drive and trip tracking.

The location data collected may include:

  • GPS location data
  • Route and path information
  • Distance traveled
  • Driving timelines

Location data is collected when the app is in use and, if permission is granted, while the app is running in the background.

AutoMiles requests "Allow all the time" (Always Allow) location permission so that driving activity can be recorded automatically even when the app is not actively open.

Location data is used solely for:

  • Automatic drive detection
  • Building driving history and route timelines
  • Mileage and distance calculation
  • Trip and vehicle logs

Location data is never sold and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Advertising and Advertising ID

AutoMiles uses third-party advertising services, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Meta Audience Network

These services may use the Android Advertising ID or similar identifiers to display personalized or non-personalized ads and to measure ad performance.

Users can reset or limit ad personalization at any time through their device settings.

Purpose of Data Collection

Data collected by AutoMiles is used only for:

  • Providing automatic drive and trip tracking
  • Maintaining driving history and records
  • Improving app performance and reliability
  • Displaying relevant advertising

AutoMiles does not sell user data.

User Control

Users can:

  • Enable or disable location access at any time via device settings
  • Choose between automatic tracking and manual drive entry
  • Reset or limit ad personalization via device settings

Data Retention

Driving history and location data are stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service to the user.

Users may delete their driving history at any time within the app.
